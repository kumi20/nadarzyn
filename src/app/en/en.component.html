LOCATION

The plot of an area of 17 000 m2 is located in the town Nadarzyn, directly off the S 8 major road connecting Warsaw with Katowice.

It has convenient access to:

  • Warsaw city center: travel time - 12 minutes
  • Exhibition Center Ptak Warsaw EXPO: travel time - 3 minutes
  • Motorway A2: travel time - 20 minutes

PREMISES

The presented plot of 17 000 m2 is private property which belongs to a natural person.

The plot has four independent entries allowing fast and smooth entry/exit of guest cars.

It is equipped with all utilities: water, electricity and wastewater.

The values of individual utilities are adjusted for full service of the presented investment.

The design was prepared in accordance with the guidelines of the Spatial Development Plan of the Nadarzyn Commune and was approved by the Office without any changes.
The project was approved by the commune of Nadarzyn and is at the stage of publication in the Journal of Laws.

INVESTMENT PROJECT

The construction of a hotel and recreation complex with conference rooms is planned on the above mentioned plot.

The project involves the construction of a hotel facility of:

  • 4 - 5 star standard
  • 550 rooms for 2 - 3 persons
  • the conference center of an area of 15 000 m2
  • wellness center - 1200 m2
  • SPA - 700 m2
  • Night Club
  • two independent restaurants
  • underground parking for 300 cars

The facility is 6 floor hight plus the dominant height in the form of a wellness center.
